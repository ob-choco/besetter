FAQ

How do I delete my account?

Open the app and go to Settings. Select "Delete Account" and type "delete" to confirm. All your data will be permanently removed.

How do I share a route?

Open a route and tap the share button. You can send the link to anyone — they can view it in the app or in a browser.

Which climbing grades are supported?

Besetter supports multiple grading systems including V-scale, Font scale, and gym-specific color grades.
